How to Host your eBay Listing Description Images for Free

Do you you want to add your own pictures to your ebay listing template?

Posted by Juan J. Martinez on November 24, 2017

One of the coolest things you can do on ebay is to add more pictures of your product on your ebay product description. Your listing description drags more atention the monent you combine pictures and words. All of this sounds preatty good but the hard part is that you have to upload your images to a separate server and write some html to add them to your desciription. This is going to take you quite a lot of time for sure. Fortunately, there are free resources like free ebay templates and imgbb.com that are going to help you create a better looking template in a very short time. For this tutorial we are going to make use of a webisite named imgbb.com to upload our very onw photos for out html template.

Uploading your First Image

The first thing you have to do in order to add your own pictures to the template is to upload them to an image hosting website. First, go to imgbb.com and click on the upload button that located on the top right corner of the website. Drag and drop the image that you want to upload to the server and click on upload. Now, this is very important. Once you upload is complete, click on the drop down menu and select the option that says "html full linked". Then, copy the link next to src= as pictured bellow.

Adding your photos the freeebaytemplates.com Web App

Once you have copied the link where your image is hosted. You just need login to  freeebaytemplates.com/app  and go though the fist two steps to create a new html listing template. On the third step you are going to find a tab that is named image carrousel. Open that tab and you are going to see five different text holders where you can paste the hyperlink of your newly uploaded image. Erase the hyperlink that is already there and paste your own link. The changes will automatically be displayed on the live preview template.
